Worlds greatest Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for the greatest Sandwich you'll ever taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can jackfruit in brine, drained
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce
  • 2 tablespoons margarine (such as Earth BalanceÂ®)
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Remove all seeds from jackfruit. Combine jackfruit and BBQ sauce in a slow cooker. Cook on High for 3 to 4 hours.
  2. Use a fork to shred jackfruit meat. Spread margarine on hamburger buns and place a scoop of jackfruit "meat" on top. Serve immediately
